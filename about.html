About Me

Hi! I'm Swaroop Chandrapalaka, a passionate Data Analyst with more than four years of experience transforming raw data into clear, actionable insights. I thrive on uncovering stories hidden in numbers and turning complex datasets into intuitive dashboards and visualizations that drive business strategy.

My toolkit includes SQL, Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib), and Power BI/Tableau. Whether it's building automated ETL pipelines or designing interactive dashboards, I’m always looking for ways to streamline processes and elevate decision‑making.

Beyond the technical skills, I bring a knack for storytelling and collaboration: translating data‑driven findings for executives, marketers, and engineers alike. I enjoy mentoring junior analysts and firmly believe in fostering a culture of continuous learning.

When I’m not crunching numbers, you can find me exploring visual design, diving into the latest AI tools, or planning my next hiking adventure.
